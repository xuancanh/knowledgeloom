Questions

The fine print, in plain type.

Is the free plan really free?

Yes. Thread stays free for as long as you want — 500 notes, the full Weave, and all four Learn formats. No card, no trial clock.

What counts as a Codex research run?

Each capture you submit for background research is one run. Thread includes 20 a month; every paid plan is unlimited.

Can I switch plans later?

Anytime. Upgrades apply instantly and prorate; downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

How does team billing work?

Atelier is billed per active seat with a 3-seat minimum. Add or remove seats whenever — we prorate the difference.

Do you offer education discounts?

Students and educators get Weaver free and 50% off Atelier. Reach out with a .edu address to enable it.

What happens to my notes if I downgrade?

Nothing is deleted. If you exceed the free note cap, older notes become read-only until you upgrade again.
