The Weave

Your knowledge, as a map of what leads to what.

Notes aren't a list — they're a network. Loom draws the prerequisite edges so you can see the shape of a subject, spot the gaps, and find the shortest path into something hard.

  • ✓ Drag from any node to link a prerequisite by hand
  • ✓ Codex proposes edges after every research pass
  • ✓ Filter by category, mastery, or recency
  • ✓ Minimap + focus mode for large vaults
